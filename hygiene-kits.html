Hygiene Kits

Jacksonville Mutual Aid Collective is assembling hygiene kits for our neighbors in need. Hygiene Kits include basic yet essential items to address hygiene and health needs such as toothbrushes, Tylenol, menstrual pads, and more.

You can get involved by bringing the bolded items below and joining us to assemble the kits on July 9th at 7pm at <Riverside Church at Park and King>.

Feel free to use this template to create your own hygiene kits to distribute within your community!
